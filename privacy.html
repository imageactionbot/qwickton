Privacy Policy

Qwickton follows a privacy-first approach with browser-side processing prioritized wherever technically possible. This policy explains what information may be handled and how it is used.

We do not require account creation for standard tool usage, and we avoid unnecessary server dependency in regular utility workflows.

Data Handling

Local Processing: tool inputs are generally processed in-browser for faster and more privacy-conscious execution.

Operational Signals: analytics and ad network scripts, where enabled, may capture aggregate or technical interaction signals to help maintain and improve platform sustainability.

Communication Data: if you contact us by email, message content is used only for support, response, and issue resolution workflows.

Cookies and Third-Party Services

Third-party services integrated for analytics or ads may use cookies or similar storage technologies according to their own policies. Users can manage such behavior through browser settings and consent controls.

Data Retention and Control

Local tool history or preferences may be stored in browser storage for convenience. Users may clear browser data at any time to remove local traces.

Security and Updates

We continuously improve runtime hardening, security headers, and page stability. This Privacy Policy may be updated over time as features, integrations, or legal requirements evolve.
