Frequently Asked Questions

How to generate a free invoice online?

Use the Invoice Generator above: enter client details, add line items, set tax rate, and click Generate. Download as TXT instantly — no signup required.

Can I use these business tools for every country?

Yes. Use the country dropdown to switch between country-specific defaults including India (GST with CGST/SGST split), US (Sales Tax), UK (VAT), and many more.

How to calculate break-even point online?

In the Break-Even Analyzer, input your fixed costs, variable cost per unit, and selling price. The tool calculates break-even units and revenue required to cover costs.

How to calculate EMI for a business loan?

The EMI Calculator takes your loan amount, annual interest rate, and tenure in months and instantly shows monthly EMI, total interest payable, and total repayment amount.

Is all data secure? Does anything go to a server?

Absolutely nothing leaves your browser. Every calculation happens client-side using JavaScript. No account, no server, no data collection.
