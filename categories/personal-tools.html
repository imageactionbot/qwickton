Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI and how is it calculated?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated as weight (kg) divided by height squared (m²).

What is BMR vs TDEE?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is calories burned at rest. TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) includes activity level and represents total daily calorie needs.

How much water should I drink daily?

The calculator uses weight-based formula (35ml/kg) plus workout and climate adjustments for personalized hydration recommendations.

Is my health data saved anywhere?

Your calculation history is saved locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to any server.

Can this be used in different countries?

Yes. Global Age formatter supports locale-aware date formats for multiple countries.
