Frequently Asked Questions

What country presets are available?

We support 40+ common country presets across Asia, Europe, North/South America, Africa, Middle East, and Oceania (including India, US, UK, Canada, UAE, Australia, Japan, Germany, France, etc.). Always verify with your official authority before final submission.

Can I edit my photo before downloading?

Yes! Upload a photo, adjust zoom, rotate, reposition by dragging, and choose background color.

How does the print sheet planner work?

It places your edited passport photo into an A4/Letter/A5 grid using your chosen margins, gap, and copy count. You can download the sheet as PNG/JPG and use the built-in PDF option (print dialog) to save as PDF.

Is my photo uploaded to any server?

No! All photo editing happens locally in your browser using Canvas API. Your photos never leave your device.
