Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter work?

Counts words, characters (with/without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, unique words, and estimates reading time.

What case conversions are supported?

UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, and InVeRt case (alternating).

What text cleaning features are available?

Remove extra spaces, join/normalize lines, slugify text, sort lines, dedupe lines, plus advanced tools like regex testing and JSON formatting.

How does text comparison work?

Compares two texts for similarity percentage, word overlap, and finds the first difference.
