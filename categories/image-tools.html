Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded to any server?

No! All image processing happens locally in your browser using Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.

What image formats are supported?

Upload: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP. Download: PNG, JPEG, WebP formats.

What filters can I apply?

In Image Lab: brightness, contrast, saturation, blur, grayscale, sepia, invert, rotate, flip — with aspect-locked resize and safe export.

Can I resize images?

Yes! Set custom width/height or use percentage scaling. Maintains aspect ratio option available.
